Platform Features
Built for Serious Research Collaboration

Everything you need to find collaborators, manage projects, and publish SDG-aligned research — in one unified platform.

🔍

Smart Researcher Discovery

Find co-researchers by SDG focus, university, skills, and publication history. AI-powered matching suggests ideal collaborators for your project goals.

🗂️

Project Management Hub

Kanban boards, milestones, version-controlled documents and shared research repositories — a GitHub-inspired workspace designed for academic teams.

📡

Live Research Feed

Follow SDG tracks like you follow topics on LinkedIn. Discover ongoing projects, preprints, datasets, and open calls for collaborators across 340+ universities.

🏛️

Institutional Profiles

Universities showcase their SDG research capacity. Professors list open positions. Students build verified academic portfolios reviewed by their institutions.

💬

Integrated Communication

Threaded discussions per project, real-time messaging, video calls, and structured meeting notes — all contextualised to your research workflow.

📈

Impact Tracking Dashboard

Quantify your SDG contribution with impact metrics, citation tracking, policy mentions, and visual reports you can share with supervisors and funders.

How UniConnect Works
From Idea to Impact in 4 Steps

The platform handles everything from initial matching to final publication — so your team can focus on the research.

01

Create Your Academic Profile

Verify your university email, list your SDG interests, skills, and prior research. Professors can add lab openings and thesis topics.

02

Discover or Post a Project

Browse active SDG projects or launch your own with defined goals, roles, and timelines. Open calls attract global co-researchers.

03

Collaborate in a Shared Workspace

Use built-in tools — version control, tasks, data sharing, and discussion threads — to run your project from start to finish.

04

Publish, Measure & Grow Your Network

Export papers, submit to journals, and track real-world SDG impact. Your contributions build a verified research portfolio.
